# 🚀 Quick Deploy to Vercel

**Your code is already on GitHub!**

## Deploy in 2 Clicks:

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com/new/git/github

2. **Click:** "Continue with GitHub"

3. **Select Repository:** `openclaw-modded-config-dashboard`

4. **Click:** "Deploy"

5. **Wait 2 minutes** - Done!

---

## Your Live URL Will Be:
`openclaw-modded-config-dashboard.vercel.app`

---

## That's It!

Vercel will:
- ✅ Build your Next.js site
- ✅ Deploy globally
- ✅ Give you HTTPS
- ✅ Auto-update on future pushes
